Installing SecurityOnion

This semester, I am taking CIT425/L alongside the rest of my courses. CIT425 is a Information and Systems Security class taught by Dr. Kaplan. In the class we explore a broad selection of systems security issues, concepts, and policies with topics within the principles of information systems security that include cryptography, identifications and authentications, access control models and mechanisms, multilevel database security, Internet security, risk analysis, and social issues that include the role privacy plays in our everyday lives in a society that has become heavily reliant on technology. In this week's blog, I will discuss how to install SecurityOnion.
